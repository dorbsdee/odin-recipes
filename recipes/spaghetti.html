Spaghetti

Make a delicious spaghetti.

You need the following:

  • Spaghetti noodles
  • Tomato sauce
  • Ground beef
  • Onion, garlic, and herbs

How to prepare:

  1. Cook the spaghetti according to package directions.
  2. Brown the ground beef in a large pan.
  3. Add the onion, garlic, and herbs to the pan and cook until fragrant.
  4. Pour in the tomato sauce and simmer for 15-20 minutes.
  5. Combine the cooked spaghetti with the meat sauce.
