Ravioli

Make a delicious ravioli.

You need the following:

  • Ravioli pack, 500 grams - you can get it from your grocery store
  • Ravioli sauce, 350 grams (or similar size) - you should get the creamy white sauce
  • Bacon or similar meat
  • Onion, garlic, and herbs

How to prepare:

  1. Boil 3liters of water in a pan.
  2. Onced boiled, place the ravioli according to package directions.
  3. Once cooked, drain the ravioli and set aside.
  4. Saute the onion, garlic, and add the bacon.
  5. Pour in the ravioli sauce and simmer for 5 minutes.
  6. Combine the cooked ravioli with the sauce and serve.
